Rules:

⇢   The game has 2 players, playing in rounds.

⇢   In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as they wish. Each result gets added to their ROUND score.

⇢   If the player rolls a 1, all their ROUND score is lost. It is then the next players turn.

⇢   The player can 'Hold' and add their ROUND score to the TOTAL score at any time. After holding, it's the next player's turn.

⇢   The first player to reach 100 points on the TOTAL score wins the game, or set your FINAL SCORE below.
